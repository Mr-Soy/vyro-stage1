MOCKING STRATEGY
----------------
- Supabase client is mocked via jest.mock() to avoid real database calls.
- faceService (detectFaces, findMatchingGrabId) is mocked in crawlService
  tests to isolate the crawl pipeline from ML model loading.
- faceModels (loadModels, tf, faceapi) is mocked in route tests to avoid
  loading TensorFlow.js and 12MB of model weights during testing.
- compareFaces() and findMatchingGrabId() are tested directly as pure
  functions with no mocking needed.
